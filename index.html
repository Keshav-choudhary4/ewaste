About E-Waste

E-waste or electronic waste is the fastest-growing waste stream globally, and India is no exception. With the rapid pace of technological advancements, electronic devices are becoming obsolete at an alarming rate, and this has led to a massive pile-up of e-waste. In addition, the increasing popularity of Li-ion batteries in electronic devices has led to a surge in the demand for these batteries, resulting in a significant increase in the production of these batteries globally.
